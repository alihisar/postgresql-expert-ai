# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participants are expected to be respectful, technically constructive, and safety-conscious.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- explaining disagreements with evidence;
- separating verified facts from operational opinion;
- acknowledging version and environment differences;
- correcting unsafe guidance quickly;
- helping contributors improve documentation quality.

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, deliberate misinformation, and unsafe production advice presented without warnings are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions that violate these standards. Serious or repeated violations may result in restricted participation.
